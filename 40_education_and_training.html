Comprehensive Coverage

Product description

Comprehensive coverage comes with a deductible, meaning you'll pay some out-of-pocket costs to fix your car and Allstate will cover the rest up to the actual cash value of your car. Deductible amounts range from $0 to $1,000. Choosing the right amount depends on how much you're comfortable spending and how much your vehicle is actually worth.
